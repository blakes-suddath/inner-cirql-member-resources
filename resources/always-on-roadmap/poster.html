The Always-On System Roadmap.

Sixteen steps from day one to a business that markets itself. Install the three systems, build your foundation with coaching, let the system run on its own, then add the amplifiers that scale it.

Install the System.
Set Up · Done Once
01Install Clara AI
Your sphere manager. Clara installs on your phone, syncs with Follow Up Boss, and works your database for you.
02Install GEO
Your generative engine optimization employee. GEO connects to your site so AI search starts citing you.
03Install Origin
Your brand engine. Origin learns your voice, then builds and maintains your online brand so it sounds like you.
04Join YourInnerCirql.com
Your community. Wins, questions, call replays, and the agents running the same system you are.

Build the Foundation.

05Coaching Call & Workshop
Get on the standing Wednesday call. We fix sticking points and work the system together, live.
06Craft Your Niche, Offer, Mechanism (3Ps)
The three decisions everything else is built on. Who you serve, what you offer, and how it works.
07Update Online Profiles with the 3Ps
Push the 3Ps to every public profile. Google, Zillow, Realtor.com, and socials all say the same thing.
08Brand Launch from Origin
Origin publishes your brand. Your story, positioning, and presence go live in your voice.
09Build Your Perfect Week
Time-block the week that makes the system run. Lead-gen, appointments, and your ten minutes with Clara each get a spot on the calendar.

Run it. Then scale it.

Phase 3 is what your business does every week without you. Phase 4 is what you launch to amplify it, once each, when you are ready.

03
Always On.
Run · Works On Its Own
10Work with Clara Daily
Ten minutes a day. Clara works your sphere, you approve and go. The habit that compounds.
11GEO Posts Blogs Twice a Week
Two posts a week, written to get you cited by AI search. You never touch a keyboard.
12Origin Audits Your Brand Weekly
A weekly audit checks your brand everywhere it lives and fixes drift before anyone notices.
04
Launch the Amplifiers.
Scale · One At A Time
13Launch Your Personal Website
Your home base, not the brokerage's. A site that turns visitors into appointments and gives Clara, GEO, and Origin one place to send everyone.
14Launch Your 5-Star Review System
Every closing becomes public proof. An automatic ask fills your Google profile with reviews while you are on to the next deal.
15Launch Your Listing Appointment Engine
One short VSL does the pre-selling. Sellers watch, warm up, and show up to the appointment ready to sign.
16Launch Email Marketing for Your Niche
One email a week to the niche from your 3Ps. You stay top of mind with the exact people you want to work with.

Install once. Show up Wednesdays. The system does the rest.
