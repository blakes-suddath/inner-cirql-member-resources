Set Up Claude Pro.

Your content engine, done right.

Claude Pro writes your GEO blog content in minutes, in your voice. Set up wrong, it sounds like generic AI. Set up right, it sounds like you and compounds into AI citations. Most agents stop at a free account and miss the part that matters. Here is the right way. Every link is clickable.

01
Create your account
Go to claude.ai and sign up with Google or your email.
WhyThis is your AI workspace. One login for everything that follows.
02
Upgrade to ProDo not skip
Open Settings, then Billing, and upgrade to Pro ($20/mo). claude.ai/settings/billing
Why it mattersFree runs out of messages fast and locks you out of the best models and Projects. Pro is the difference between a toy and a content engine.
03
Switch on the best model
In the model menu at the top, pick the most capable model, not the default fast one.
Why it mattersThe top model writes the most natural, GEO-ready content. The wrong model is the number one reason agents get mediocre output.
04
Create a ProjectDo not skip
Open Projects and create one called My GEO Blog. claude.ai/projects
Why it mattersA Project remembers your voice, market, and specialty across every chat, so you never re-explain yourself. This is the step almost everyone skips.

05
Load your brand into the ProjectDo not skip
Paste your bio, your market, your specialty, your entity statement, and a few samples of your writing into the Project knowledge.
Why it mattersThis is how Claude writes in your voice instead of generic AI. Skip it and your blog reads like everyone else's.
06
Get the apps
Install the desktop and mobile app so Claude is one click away. claude.ai/download
Why it mattersEasy access is what makes the daily content habit actually stick.
07
Write your first post
Inside your Project, ask Claude to draft a GEO blog post that answers a real buyer question for your market.
Why it mattersThis is the payoff. Every post you publish becomes a new chance for AI to cite you by name.
Why you cannot skip this
Claude Pro is the engine. The upgrade, the Project, and your voice are what make it yours. Get those three right and every post sounds like you and works while you sleep.
