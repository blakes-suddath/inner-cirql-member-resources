Claude 101. Install Guide.
For Real Estate Agents

How to Install Claude.

Six easy steps. Five minutes. No tech experience needed.

Step 01
Go to claude.ai
Type claude.ai in your browser. Hit enter.
Step 02
Click Sign Up.
Look top-right. Click the button.
Step 03
Pick how to join.
Use Google, or type your email.
Step 04
Confirm it's you.
Google: click your account. Email: type the code they send.
Step 05
Add your name.
Type your first name. Click continue.
Step 06
Ask a question.
Type. Hit enter. Claude answers.
