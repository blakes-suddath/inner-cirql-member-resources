GBP Setup Guide
For Real Estate Agents

Set up your Google Business Profile.

Step by step, start to finish.

No profile yet, or you never finished setup? Follow these seven steps to get a verified profile live. Tap any gold link to jump straight to the page.

01
Get a Google account
If you do not have one yet, create a free Google account. accounts.google.com/signup
02
Open Business Profile Manager
Start a brand new profile here. business.google.com/create
03
Name your business
Use your full professional name, exactly as on your license. Example: Sarah Chen, REALTOR®.
04
Choose your category
Set the primary business category to Real estate agent.
05
Set your service area
Most agents have no public office. Turn off the storefront address and add the cities and ZIP codes you serve.
06
Add your contact info
Add a local phone number and your website.
07
Verify your profile
Follow the prompts to verify, usually a short video. Until you verify, you stay invisible. Stuck? support.google.com/business
